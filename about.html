Hi, I'm Jovin Aganon and I'm currently a Web Design student at Southwest CTA in Las Vegas, Nevada. I'm a sociable guy who loves to meet new people and I'm ready to take on any task you throw at me.

After 4 years of living in Vegas, I've just about completed my junior year in high school and I'm one step closer to stepping out into the real world.
