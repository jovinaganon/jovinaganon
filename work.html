  • Creating a face out of different pictures in Adobe Photoshop was rather challenging, but fun. Being allowed a choice of theme, I decided to go with the theme of golf, which is a hobby of mine. I searched up a lot of different golf materials, from golf clubs to golf courses. I ended up using the back face of a driver to serve the base of the face. After more searching, I found myself using a tree and grass as the hair, a lake and two courses as the eyebrows and mouth, then golf balls for the eye and golf clubs as the teeth. Though just sticking them together wouldn't do it justice. I blended the items together to make a face by using the dodge and burn tools to darken the values of shadows and brighten places where light would be shining on it. After about a week, I had put together a funky looking face!
